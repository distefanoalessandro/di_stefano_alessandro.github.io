Alessandro Di Stefano

Lecturer in Computer Science, Department of Computer Science and Information Systems, Teesside University, Middlesbrough, UK

About me and my research

I am a lecturer in Computer Science at the Department of Computer Science and Information Systems, Teesside University, Middlesbrough, UK. I've an interdisciplinary approach to research which incorporates engineering and computer science methodologies applied to socio-technological systems. My research interests include evolutionary game theory, complex networks, human cooperation, social behaviours, homophily, mobile crowdsensing, decision-making models, epidemic spreading, ICT and machine learning.

Details of my publications can be found on my Google Scholar profile or my publications page
